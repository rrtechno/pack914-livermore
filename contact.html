How to Join — 4 Easy Steps

1

Fill Out the Form →

Use the contact form on the right to introduce yourself, or go straight to Scouting.org to register.

2

Attend a Meeting

We'll invite you to a Pack or den meeting. Come see what we're all about — no commitment needed!

3

Register Online

Complete your BSA registration at Scouting.org. Annual dues apply.

4

Get Uniformed & Start!

Pick up your uniform from the Scout Shop and you're officially a Cub Scout!
