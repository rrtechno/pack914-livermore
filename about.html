Our Story

Pack 914 — A Livermore Tradition

Cub Scout Pack 914 has been serving the youth and families of Livermore, California for many years, providing a safe, fun, and enriching environment where young people can grow into confident, caring, and capable individuals. The Pack is sponsored by the American Legion Post 47 of Livermore.

We are a family-run, volunteer-led Pack chartered through the Scouting America and part of the Twin Valley District, within the Golden Gate Area Council. Our Pack welcomes boys and girls in Kindergarten through 5th grade — no experience required, just a willingness to try new things!

We believe Scouting builds character that lasts a lifetime. Our scouts learn leadership, teamwork, and responsibility — all while having the time of their lives on camping trips, at the Pinewood Derby, and in service to our community.

Our Mission

To Build Character in the Youth of Livermore

Pack 914 partners with families and the community to develop the qualities of leadership, ethical decision-making, physical fitness, and good citizenship in every Scout — guided always by the Scout Oath and Scout Law.

The Foundation of Scouting

Scout Oath & Scout Law

Scout Oath

On my honor I will do my best
To do my duty to God and my country
and to obey the Scout Law;
To help other people at all times;
To keep myself physically strong,
mentally awake, and morally straight.
